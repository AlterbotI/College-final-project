TECHNICAL EDUCATION IN PAKISTAN

"The significance of Technicina and Technical Education for economy of a country is prominent due to the industrial and revolution which have changed the world face.The growth, production and proper function of an industry largely depends on the pyramids of productive work force i.e Engineer.Associate Engineer and skill worker An Associate engineer occupies the most important and middile position in the pyramid of the productive work force .Though the skill worker on Associate Engineer shapes up the ideas of engineer into reality.The main source of production of Assoicate Engineers are college technologies,Polytechnics and Technical Institutes Soon after the independence of pakistan in 1947 it was discoverd that there was an acute shortage of technical institutes in the country In view of the goverment constituted"council of Technical Eduction for Pakistan" in june 1948.The Council recommended a scheme of polytechnics,monotechnics and Technical Insititutes with a post matric three year Diploma of Associate Engineer (D.A.E) Course in september,1950 To accelerate the development of Technical Education,In 1958 the directorate of technical education was setup at lahore.The commision on national Education (December 1958) recoginzed the importance of Technical Education for economical progress and defined its place in the system of education.By the end of 1962 it became clear on that only one direction of Technical Education for the whole country of Pakistan is inadequate.Therefore another Directorate of Technical Rducation was established at karachi in feb,1964.Presently all provinces have setup their own boards of technical education with their respective names.Technical and vocational training authority has been constituted to over sight technical education in Pakistan.


" HASANI COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY "

"Madam I B Hasani Founder of "Hasani College Of Technology".She was born in a learnd family in 1933 .After her education society in1956 named "Hasani Literary Society".The Hasani Literary Society established following institution:- A) Hasani Boys and Girls Primary and Secondary school at Fridous Colony in 1956 B) Mehboob Technical Institute at Firdous Colony in 1960. C) Hasani Children Home North Nazimabad in sadder over United Bakery and Primary section shifted in Block"S"in North Nazimabad All above mentiond schools nationalized by the goverment in 1972 Later Goverment reveiewed education policy and invited private sector to practicipate development of education Madam I.B Hasani emphasized on Technical Education keeping in view thath "skilled person never unemployed" and started "Hasani Technical High School" in block-18,F.B.Area,recognized by "Sindh Board of Technical Eduation" in 1948 and upgrade it as "Hasani Institute of Technology" in Block-18 F.B Area on 3.5 arces area,recognized and affiliated with "Sindh Board of Technical Education and STEVTA" conducting courses of "3 year Diploma Of Associate Engineer" in the disciplines of the Electrical,Electronics and Civil Technology in 2008, college was upgraded as Hasani College of Technology with addition of three more technologies, Mechanical,Telecommunication and Computer information technology,further in 2018 Auto & Diesel Technology was introduced to provide qulifed manpower for the Automotive industry .This College is inculded in renowned Technical colleges of Pakistan.
